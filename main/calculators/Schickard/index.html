Alan's Schickard Calculator page

My home built Schickard Calculator:

As with most of my projects, it was a sudden and ill-thought-out decision to undertake it. Rather than studying or planning first what I intend to do, I tend to jump in and make a start. So, this project is not historically accurate, but is loosely based on information kindly provided by Jens Puhle HERE. I adapted it to be easy and cheap for me to build, using materials that are easily available, mostly MDF, and using my homemade CNC router.
I first learned of the existence of the Schickard Calculator on November 10th, and by November 29th, I had finished building my own which It has to be some kind of record?
