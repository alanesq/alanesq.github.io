My mechanical calculator collection

I have a collection of mechanical calculators that I have all restored to full working order. My interest in these machines began when I saw a YouTube video featuring a Friden calculator. After telling someone about my newfound interest, I was gifted an adding machine for my birthday. Later someone else gifted me a couple and this resulted in my aquiring more and more of them until I now have around 15.
