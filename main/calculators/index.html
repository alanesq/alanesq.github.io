Alan's Mechanical Calculator Page

My collection of old adding machines

I have a collection of mechanical calculators that I have rescued and restored to full working order. My interest in these machines began when I stumbled upon a YouTube video featuring a Friden calculator. After telling someone about my newfound interest, they gifted me an adding machine for my birthday. Soon after, someone else gifted me a couple more and this eventually led to my acquiring more and more of them until I now have around 15. These machines seem to attract little interest or value, (apart from the Curta that is), many of them have been given to me or I have bought for under Â£20. I like to think I am rescuing them from being lost and maybe interest in them will increase someday
