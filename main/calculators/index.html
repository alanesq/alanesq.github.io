Alan's Mechanical Calculator Page

My collection of old adding machines

I have a collection of mechanical calculators that I have rescued and returned to full working order. My interest in these machines began when I happened to see a YouTube video featuring a Friden calculator. After telling someone about my newfound interest, I was later gifted an adding machine for my birthday. Later someone else gifted me a couple and this resulted in my acquiring more and more of them until I now have around 15.
