Welcome to my guide on how to easily and cheaply make yourself an English longbow, requiring no previous skills or experience.

Due to the amount of money I found I was spending on longbows only to have them break on me I decided to see if I could cut down on costs by making my own. 
I soon found that it is much less difficult than I expected to make a working bow and it did save me a small fortune.
I later decided to create this guide to pass on what I have learned along the way, to show you that it is something you too can do (link below).
