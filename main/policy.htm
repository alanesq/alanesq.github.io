Shops, companies and the like often seem to throw the excuse at me that "this is a company policy and so can not be discussed any further".  
Whilst this annoyed me a great deal, I later realised that this is actually a very powerful scheme and so I will buy into it myself. 
So here I publicly declare some policies of my own:

The policies listed below are my personal policies. and as such the decisions on these have already been fully considered and decided upon, no further discussion or consideration will be undertaken on an individual case basis. 
These policies are reviewed on an annual basis, if you feel a change is required then email your request to alanesq@disroot.org and it will be put in front of a committee (of no fewer than 4 people, selected by Alan) who will consider your case at the next scheduled review.  I will aim to inform you of the decision within 60 days of the review.


1. I will never under any circumstances work on any kind of helpdesk/call centre (i.e. answering phones)

2. I will not have anything to do with animal derived products or services which rely on them (other than for serious medical purposes*)

3. I never queue for anything (not more than a couple of mins anyway)

4. I will not attand a "dinner party" of any kind.

5. I will not take part in any activity which involves dancing or singing.

6. I will not take anything simply on authority, only by logical, reasoned evidence based argument.

This comprises a brief outline of my policies, others may be added at any time so please refer back to this page on a regular basis. 


* Serious medical purposes are things which are more than just an irritation or cosmetic and where no effective alternative is available   (see HERE for explanation)
